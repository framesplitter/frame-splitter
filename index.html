Canva App

Frame Splitter

Frame Splitter helps you detect and split individual frames from a storyboard image, then place the extracted frames into your Canva design in order.

The app is designed for storyboard sheets, visual direction boards, AI-generated storyboard images, and other frame-based image layouts.

Detect frames Automatically detects frame areas from a single storyboard image.
Fine-tune manually Adjust detection boxes manually when needed.
Place in Canva Place extracted frames into your Canva design in order.
Privacy Policy Terms of Use Support

Frame Splitter does not use generative AI, does not require account registration, and does not store uploaded images on external servers.
