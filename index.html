Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Frame Splitter handles information when you use the app. Frame Splitter is a Canva app that detects and splits frames from storyboard images.

Information we collect

Frame Splitter does not collect personal information. The app does not require account registration and does not ask users to provide names, email addresses, or other personal details.

Uploaded images

Images uploaded to Frame Splitter are used only for frame detection, splitting, previewing, manual adjustment, and placement into the user’s Canva design.

Uploaded images are processed within the app and are not stored on our external servers.

Generative AI

Frame Splitter does not use generative AI. Uploaded images are not used for AI training, model training, analytics, marketing, advertising, or third-party sharing.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data or uploaded images with third parties.

Data retention

Frame Splitter does not retain uploaded images on external servers. Any images or generated frame previews are used only during the active app session for the purpose of providing the app’s core functionality.

Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app safe and limit data handling to what is necessary for the app to function.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
